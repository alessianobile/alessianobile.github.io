No 1 About the Project

I worked on this project during my internship at Illimity Bank, the first fully-digital bank in Italy. I designed the entire experience for a partnership between the bank and Azimut, an investing company whose clients had access to a special version of the Illimity online banking service.

No 2 Project Description

An Azimut consultant presents his assigned client a new valid solution for his business, offering Illimity Bank's services. Trust always plays a huge part in the relationship between the consultant and his client, who must feel that the offers are always tailored for him and his interests.

The text of the email is short and minimal, because the reception of the email always follows a first meeting between the client and the consultant. A summary picture placed in the middle of the page illustrates the services offered by Illimity to Azimut's customer.

By clicking on the button in the email, the customer will access the designed landing page. At any time the Azimut investor can click on the chat button to chat with his consultant and ask for further information about the offer.

Scrolling down the webpage he can actually see what the Illimity Bank homepage looks like.

All the other parts of the Illimity app were adapted thorugh custom designs: for instance the main menu, the accont recap and the chat.
