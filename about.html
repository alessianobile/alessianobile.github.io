No 2 Experience

I am currently working as a UX/UI Designer at UX Boutique, an italian design agency that creates innovative designs for prestigious companies ranging from fashion giants to awarded startups.

Before that, I worked as UX/UI Design Intern at Banco BPM, one of the biggest italian banks. I tested the two BPM banking apps, one made for private customers and the other one created for investors and I proposed some improvements for them.

In 2019, I worked as UX/UI Intern at Illimity Bank, the first Italian fully-digital bank. I designed the interfaces of core sections of website and mobile application in a fast-paced team, testing the system in close contact with developers and data analysts. My work contributed to the very successful first launch of the product.

No 1 Education

In 2019, I took my Master's Degree in Design in Milan, learning about both theoretical and practical aspects of UX Design. Completing several projects helped me understand what working in this field feels like, and how resourcefulness, creativity and commitment are so important in order to grow. I defended a thesis on the importance of user trust when designing interfaces for online banking apps, based on a parallelism between physical and digital banking.

Previously, I completed a Bachelor's Degree in Communication Sciences in Catania, my hometown, in 2017. My thesis was focused on Human-Machine Interaction and Interaction Design as a way to identify the users' needs and to create intuitive interfaces: it helped me build my interest in User Experience and Design in general.
