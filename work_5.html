No 1 About the Project

Design of the "Innovative ideas" page of a social platform. The page was provided by the bank in order to increase the customer engagement by giving the users the chance to express their ideas on what the bank of the future should be, and by allowing them to cooperate with the bank itself.

Four different types of personalities were created to represent the idea of "illimiter", the typical user of the new online banking service: the pioneer, the visionary, the enthusiast and the magnetic. A pioneer is a person who tends to explore and set important standars for the world, while a visionary is someone who always likes to see over the edges; an enthusiast is always positive about life and change, and a magnetic is a person that always surrounds himself with people and always knows how to amaze and inspire them.

The page was designed for users to post the pictures that better represent their idea of the four different personalities.

No 2 Project Description

The page allows the user to navigate among the pictures from the different categories, and every category has a specific color and icon.

Every picture is inserted into a card that shows the title chosen by the author and the name of the author, and all the members of the community can click like on their favorite posts and write comments.

By clicking on every card it's possible to see the single photo and have access to the comment section.
